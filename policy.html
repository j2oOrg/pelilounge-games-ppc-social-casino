This Privacy Policy explains how Pelilounge collects and uses information when you use the site.

Free-play only. No real money, prizes, cash-out, or purchases are available.

    We do not use third-party analytics or ad trackers.

  3. How We Use Data

    We use information to respond to direct messages and keep the site running.

    If you email us directly, we respond directly to your message.

  4. Sharing

    We do not sell personal information. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.

    We only share information if required by law.

  5. Users Under 18 or 21

    This site is intended only for adults who meet local age requirements (18+). In some locations the minimum age may be 21.

    We do not knowingly collect personal information from users below the required minimum age. If you believe a minor has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

  6. Retention

    We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

    We do not receive or store on-page form submissions on our servers.

  7. Security

    We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no system is 100 percent secure.

    Please avoid sharing sensitive information by email.

  8. Your Rights

    You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

    You can also clear local storage in your browser at any time.

  9. Contact

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@pelilounge.games.

    We reply by email when we can.

  10. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the site means you accept the updated policy.

    When we update the policy, we will revise the date at the top of this page.
